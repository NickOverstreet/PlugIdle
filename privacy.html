PlugIdle Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 9, 2026

PlugIdle is an idle game made by Nick Overstreet. This policy explains what information the game handles, in plain language. The short version: PlugIdle has no accounts, no sign-in, and does not collect, store, or sell your personal data. The Android app shows optional, opt-in rewarded ads served by Google AdMob, and offers optional in-app purchases processed by Google Play.

Game saves

Your progress is stored only on your device (in the app's local storage / IndexedDB). It is never uploaded to us — we have no servers and no way to see it. You can erase it at any time with the in-game Hard Reset, by clearing the site's data in your browser, or by uninstalling the Android app. The optional export/import save code exists so you can move progress between devices; it never passes through us.

Advertising (Android app only)

The Android version of PlugIdle shows rewarded ads — short videos you can choose to watch in exchange for an in-game bonus. Ads are never forced and never interrupt play. Ads are served by Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, Google may process device information such as your advertising ID, IP address, approximate (coarse) location, and ad-interaction data, as described in Google's privacy & terms for partners and Google's privacy policy.

If you are in a region that requires it (such as the EEA, UK, or Switzerland), the app asks for your consent before showing ads, using Google's consent flow. You can also reset your advertising ID or opt out of ad personalization in your Android settings (Settings → Google → Ads).

In-app purchases (Android app only)

Optional purchases (such as boosts, time warps, themes, and the supporter pack) are processed entirely by Google Play. We never see or store your payment details. What you have purchased is recorded in your local save and with Google Play so it can be restored on a new device. Purchases are governed by Google Play's terms of service; refunds follow Google Play's refund policy.

The web version

The web version at nickoverstreet.github.io/pwa-webapp contains no ads, no analytics, and no trackers. It is hosted on GitHub Pages; GitHub may log standard technical request data (like IP addresses) to operate the service, per GitHub's privacy statement. Fonts and all other assets are served from the same site — no third-party requests are made.

Children

PlugIdle is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, children included. Ads in the Android app are requested in a manner appropriate to a general audience.

Your choices

  • Don't want ads? Simply never tap a rewarded-ad button — the game is fully playable without them. The web version has no ads at all.
  • Want your data gone? Hard Reset in-game or uninstall — everything lives on your device.
  • Consent given for ads can be changed via the privacy options in the app's settings.

Changes & contact

If this policy changes (for example when new features ship), the updated version will be posted at this address with a new effective date. Questions or concerns: nickcoverstreet@gmail.com.
