PlugIdle Data Deletion

Effective date: June 23, 2026

PlugIdle has no accounts and no sign-in, and stores no personal data on any server we control. Here is how to delete the data the game touches.

Your game saves

Your progress is stored only on your device. You can erase it yourself at any time, instantly and completely, by:

  • using the in-game Hard Reset in Settings, or
  • uninstalling the app, or
  • clearing the site's data in your browser (web version).

We have no servers and no copy of your save, so there is nothing for us to delete on our end.

Advertising data (Android & iOS apps)

The apps show optional rewarded ads served by Google AdMob. To serve ads, Google may process device information such as your advertising ID, IP address, approximate location, and ad-interaction data. You can reset or delete this identifier yourself in your device settings (Android: Settings → Google → Ads; iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking / Apple Advertising), and manage Google's data via Google's My Activity and Google's privacy policy.

Request deletion from us

If you would like us to help delete any data associated with your use of PlugIdle, email nickcoverstreet@gmail.com with the subject "PlugIdle data deletion request". Because we hold no account or server-side personal data, we will confirm there is nothing on our side to remove and point you to the device and Google controls above for the advertising data.
